Why use an Online Mouse Jiggler?

Working from home (WFH) comes with challenges, and one of them is the annoying "Away" status on Microsoft Teams, Slack, Skype, or Outlook. If you step away for 5 minutes, your status turns yellow, giving the impression that you aren't working.

OrbitWebTools solves this issue. It is a "Mouse Jiggler Online" that prevents your screen from going to sleep.

How it works:

Unlike fake mouse movers that physically move your cursor, this website uses the HTML5 WakeLock API and a hidden video loop. This signals your operating system that "activity" is happening, preventing the sleep mode trigger.
